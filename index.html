WE ARE

- PROFUND FUNDATION -

ProFund Fundation is a professional and blockchain-focused venture capital firm in Asia. Founded in Singapore in 2018 by senior investors and artisan across both blockchain and traditional financial industries, it has to date supported several leading projects across continents leveraging blockchain technology to reshape myriad industries such as finance, healthcare, supply chain, and consumer goods. Our mission is to drive healthy and sustainable growth in the global blockchain ecosystem, and actively works as a long-term strategic partner to its portfolio projects.
